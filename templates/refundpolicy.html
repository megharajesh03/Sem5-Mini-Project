Refund Policy

Kindly read the following very carefully!


At The Getaway Mansion, we strive to ensure every guest enjoys a remarkable experience. If, for any reason, you're not fully satisfied with your stay, we're committed to working with you to find a solution.


Refund Eligibility:


Cancellations: Cancellations made 72 hours or more prior to the arrival date are eligible for a full refund, minus any applicable processing fees. Cancellations made within 72 hours of the arrival date will forfeit the first night's room rate. No-shows or early departures will be charged the full amount of the reservation.


Shortened Stays: If you depart early for reasons beyond our control, we'll refund the remaining nights of your stay, excluding the first night and any applicable processing fees.


Refundable Rates: Only reservations booked under refundable rates are eligible for refunds.


Refund Process:


Contact us immediately to initiate the refund process. Provide your booking confirmation number and reason for cancellation or refund request. We'll review your request and process your refund within 5-7 business days.


Non-Refundable Circumstances:


Special Events or Packages: Certain packages or events may have specific refund policies. Please refer to the terms and conditions of your booking for details.
Force Majeure: Refunds may not be granted for cancellations or disruptions due to events beyond our control, such as natural disasters, government restrictions, or unforeseen circumstances.


Contact Us:


If you have any questions or concerns about our Refund Policy, please contact us.

This policy is subject to change without notice.
We reserve the right to make decisions regarding refunds on a case-by-case basis.
All refunds will be processed in the original form of payment.
