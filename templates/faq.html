F.A.Q

Kindly read the following very carefully!


Booking & Cancellations:


What types of rooms do you offer? We offer a variety of luxurious suites and villas, each with unique amenities and stunning views. Please visit our website for detailed descriptions and photos.

What is your cancellation policy? Our cancellation policy varies depending on the rate you booked. You can find the specific details on your booking confirmation email.

Can I modify my reservation? Yes, please contact our reservations team and we'll be happy to assist you.

Do you offer group discounts? Absolutely! Contact our reservations team for information on group rates and packages.


Amenities & Services:


What amenities are included in my stay? All our guests enjoy access to our infinity pool, spa, fitness center, private beach, and a variety of restaurants and bars. Certain room types may offer additional amenities like private pools or butler service.

Do you offer room service? Yes, we offer 24-hour room service for your convenience.

Do you have childcare services? Yes, we offer supervised childcare activities and babysitting services upon request.

Do you have accessible rooms? We are committed to providing accessibility for all guests. Please inquire about our accessible rooms at the time of booking.


General Information:


Do you allow pets? Yes, we are a pet-friendly hotel.

What is your smoking policy? Smoking is strictly prohibited inside the hotel, but designated outdoor smoking areas are available..
