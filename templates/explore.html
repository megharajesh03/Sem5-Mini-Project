Luxurious Rooms

Our luxurious rooms at Getaway Mansion redefine the concept of opulence. Each room is a sanctuary of elegance, meticulously designed to envelop you in comfort and style. From plush furnishings to breathtaking views, every detail is crafted to create an unforgettable experience. Welcome to your haven of serenity and indulgence.

Gym center

At Getaway Mansion, our gym center is your gateway to wellness. Discover a world of modern fitness equipment and a serene environment designed to invigorate your body and mind. Embrace a healthier, more energetic you during your stay.

Restaurant

Savor the art of culinary excellence at Getaway Mansion's dining restaurant. Immerse yourself in a symphony of flavors created by our expert chefs, amidst an ambiance that exudes elegance and sophistication. Every meal is a journey of taste, where exquisite cuisine meets impeccable service.

Make a splash in the ultimate aquatic playground at Getaway Mansion. Our waterpark is a thrilling haven of water slides, lazy rivers, and family fun, all set against the backdrop of our luxurious retreat. It's where unforgettable memories are made under the sun and by the pool.

Explore a mesmerizing underwater world at Getaway Mansion's breathtaking aquariums. Immerse yourself in the beauty of marine life as you wander through captivating displays that bring the ocean's wonders to your fingertips. A truly unique and enchanting experience awaits within our hotel's walls.

Discover pure bliss and rejuvenation at Getaway Mansion's wellness spas. Step into a world of relaxation and self-care, where expert therapists pamper you with indulgent treatments and therapies.
